Chicken Stir Fry

A chicken stir fry loaded with vegetables and with a unique sauc.

ingredient list

  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cups brocoli
  • 1/2 yellow bell pepper
  • 1/2 red bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup baby carrots
  • 2 tsp minced ginger
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • salt and pepper

Stir fry sauce

  • 1 tbsp corn starch
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 tbsp sasame oil
  • 1/2 tsp crushed re pepper flakes

Cooking procedure

  1. In a bowl whisk together corn starch and water. Add remaining sauce ingredients and whisk together. Set aside.
  2. Add one tbsp of olive oil to skillet and heat over medium-high.
  3. Add chicken and season with salt and pepper. Cook for 3-5 min until cooked through and set aside.
  4. reduce heat to medium and remaining olive oil to skillet.
  5. Add brocoli, bell pepper, and carrots and cook while stirring occasionally, just unitl tender. Add ginger and garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  6. Add chicken back to skillet and stir to combine.
  7. Whisk stir fry sauce and pour into skillet and stir gently to combine.
  8. Bring to boil, stirring occasionally, and let boil for 1 minute.
